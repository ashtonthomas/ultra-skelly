# Vision

What kind of experience do we want the skeleton to deliver on Halloween night?

## Target experiences

_Examples to explore: reacts to trick-or-treaters approaching, holds a short improvised conversation, comments on costumes, tells a joke, reads a "fortune", reacts to group size, remembers repeat visitors within the night._

-

## Audience

_Outdoor porch/driveway, weather, ambient lighting, street noise, power access, distance from WiFi._

## Tone / persona

_Spooky-scary, playful-spooky, campy, wise-oracle, irreverent? One persona or selectable?_
